Alumni Notes

  • SEAS alum Subir Parulekar—A leader in streaming
    5/8/24

    For more than a decade, he has worked for Netflix and Disney, helping develop platforms that deliver countless hours of entertainment—a career that he prepared for as a student at UB.

  • Victor Bahl to receive 2024 Dean’s Award for Achievement
    5/7/24

    Bahl (MS ’88, BS ’86) will return to the University at Buffalo to accept the Dean’s Award for Achievement and address the class of 2024. 

  • Three SEAS alumni elevated to university leadership positions
    2/2/24

    The three add to a long list of alumni who currently hold prominent positions at academic institutions.

  • UB designated a National Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Research through 2028
    12/11/23

    The distinction has been held since 2009.

  • SEAS alum receives 20-Year Test of Time Award at STOC
    10/24/23

    D. Sivakumar's paper was honored for ushering in a new chapter in lattice algorithms, adding the powerful new technique of sieving to the field.

  • Agrusa makes historic $40 million gift to SEAS, largest in school history
    10/13/23

    Longtime School of Engineering and Applied Sciences benefactor and alum, Russell L. Agrusa, has made a $40 million commitment to support engineering and computer science education and the construction of a new SEAS building that will serve as a hub for student learning and activity.

  • LSAMP, renewed for another 5 years, ‘opens the door’ for SEAS underrepresented students
    2/22/23

    Thanks to a $2.5 million grant renewal, the program will continue to support students of color pursuing STEM degrees.

  • University at Buffalo mourns the loss of our alumnus, friend and benefactor, John R. 'Jack' Davis
    2/8/23

    A 1955 graduate of UB’s School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, Jack believed deeply in the transformative power of an education.
