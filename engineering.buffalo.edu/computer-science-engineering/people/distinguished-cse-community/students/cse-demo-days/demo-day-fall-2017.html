Demo Day—Fall 2017

Demo Day Fall 2017 participants gather in the Davis Hall atrium.

Demo Day Fall 2017 participants gather in the Davis Hall atrium

We're thrilled to invite you to the third anual Comp. Sci. & Eng. Fall Demo Day.  Student groups from several CSE capstone classes will be presenting the culmination of 3-months of effort, hard work, (metaphorical) blood, sweat (well... caffeine really), and tears (see above). 

Best Demos

Best Presentation

Just-in-Time Datastructure Modeling, by Darshana Balakrishnan

Most Impactful (Tie)

Vanir: Probabilistic Databases on Spark, by Nicholas Cellino and William Spoth​

Most Impactful (Tie)

Differentially Private Sparse Inverse Covariance Estimation, by Mengdi Huai and Di Wang
