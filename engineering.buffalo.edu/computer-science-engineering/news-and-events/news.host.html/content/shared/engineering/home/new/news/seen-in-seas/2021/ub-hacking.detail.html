UB Hacking returns to Davis Hall

UB Hacking returned to an in-person format this year, with over 300 enthusiastic students convening in Davis Hall for the 24-hour event and more from outside UB participated remotely. Students worked in teams of up to four people to develop a project in 24 hours, and 88 teams submitted projects. The student-run event was held November 6-7 inside Davis Hall.

This year’s sponsors included M&T Tech, ACV Auctions, Foundry, Moog, Wegmans, ValueCentric/IQVIA, Teknic, Stark & Wayne, and Willis Towers Watson. Partner organizations were CSExplore (donated hardware and ran the hardware lab), CMI AV, and StickerMule. More information is available on the UB Hacking website.
