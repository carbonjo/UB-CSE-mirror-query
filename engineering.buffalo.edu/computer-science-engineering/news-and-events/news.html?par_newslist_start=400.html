News

  • Image of Palestinian carrying children out of rubble shows signs of AI
    11/1/23

    Agence France-Presse featured Siwei Lyu (CSE) in a fact-checking article on an image of a man carrying children through rubble that has been shared tens of thousands of times on social media in posts linking it to a bombing in Gaza. 

  • 5 Best Blockchain Developer Courses and Certifications
    10/26/23

    Analytics Insight spotlighted UB as one of the best places offering online Blockchain courses. UB’s Blockchain ThinkLab is led by Bina Ramamurthy.

  • SEAS alum receives 20-Year Test of Time Award at STOC
    10/24/23

    D. Sivakumar's paper was honored for ushering in a new chapter in lattice algorithms, adding the powerful new technique of sieving to the field.

  • UB researchers discuss AI’s role in education
    10/23/23

    Panel discussion at Grand Island High School was part of a chat series that explores AI for social good.

  • Debunked: YouTube ads feature Elon Musk in deepfake videos pushing cryptocurrency scams
    10/20/23

    Siwei Lyu was featured in a fact-checking article from The Journal (Ireland) about a video of Elon Musk pushing cryptocurrency trading schemes. 

  • UB takes AI chat series to Grand Island Central School District
    10/18/23

    Educators will discuss how to best utilize artificial intelligence in the classroom.

  • Understanding Artificial Intelligence in schools to help students with learning disabilities
    10/13/23

    WKBW-TV, WIVB-TV and Niagara Frontier Publications reported on “Advancing Education with Responsible AI,” the second in a series of panel discussions for the UB | AI Chat Series. 

  • Agrusa makes historic $40 million gift to SEAS, largest in school history
    10/13/23

    Longtime School of Engineering and Applied Sciences benefactor and alum, Russell L. Agrusa, has made a $40 million commitment to support engineering and computer science education and the construction of a new SEAS building that will serve as a hub for student learning and activity.

  • How to stop AI deepfakes from sinking society — and science
    9/27/23

    Siwei Lyu was quoted by Nature in an article on the negative impact that AI deepfakes could have on elections, the stock market and reputations. 

  • Fact check: Fake Zelenskyy video raises fears of AI disinfo
    9/26/23

    Siwei Lyu confirms the video of Ukrainian president Volodymyr Zelensky belly dancing is fake.
