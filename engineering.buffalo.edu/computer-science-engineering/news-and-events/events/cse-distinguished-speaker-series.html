CSE Distinguished Speaker Series

CSE Distinguished Speakers Series (DSS), 2023-2024

The CSE Distinguished Speakers Series (DSS) invites internationally-recognized scholars to share their most innovative and exciting work with the CSE community.

DSS talks are hosted in Barbara and Jack Davis Hall, Room 101, on selected Thursdays from 3:30pm to 5:00pm.

DSS talks are free and open to the public.
