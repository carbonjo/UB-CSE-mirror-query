Transportation and Parking

Transportation Guidelines
  • Guests staying at the Marriott and DoubleTree may use the free shuttle at the Center for Tomorrow (CFT) located near the corner of Maple Rd and Flint Rd.
  • Guests staying at Staybridge may use the free shuttle located near the corner of Sweet Home Rd and Rensch Rd.
  • Guests with special transportation needs can inquire by contacting the Department or by visiting the registration desk.
General Parking Instructions
  • Free parking on campus is available for the Thursday evening reception and for the events on Saturday and Sunday.
Parking on Friday, September 29th
  • Parking passes will be distributed to attendees on Friday morning outside the Center for the Arts. Guests should pull up to the main entrance where staff will be distributing parking passes. Please arrive early as parking may take time.
Transportation to Glen Oak on Friday, September 29th
  • A shuttle bus will be provided for Friday evening’s event at Glen Oak Golf Course. The shuttle will pick up guests at the Marriott hotel at 4:50pm. Guests staying at the Doubletree should walk to the Marriott to catch this shuttle. The shuttle will pick up guests at StayBridge at 5:00pm and stop in front of the Center for the Arts at 5:15pm. The shuttle will leave Glen Oak at 9pm to return guests to their hotels.
Instructions for the Bus Tour of Buffalo
  • Those registered for the bus tour of Buffalo can board the bus at 10am at the Center for the Arts. The bus will return to the Center for the Arts at 4:30pm.
