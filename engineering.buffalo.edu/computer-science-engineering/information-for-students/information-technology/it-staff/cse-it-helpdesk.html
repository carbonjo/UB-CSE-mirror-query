CSE IT Helpdesk

On this page:

Services

The CSE student-staffed CSE IT Helpdesk provides these services:

  1. General PC consulting.
  2. Operating System consulting (UNIX, Linux, MAC, Windows).
  3. Reasonable academic computing help requests.

Daily To-Do List

The CSE IT Helpdesk completes these daily duties on weekdays:

  1. Check the CSE Copy/Mail Room
    • Stock paper, clear any jams and check toner for:
      • groucho
      • hardy
      • monk (copier)
