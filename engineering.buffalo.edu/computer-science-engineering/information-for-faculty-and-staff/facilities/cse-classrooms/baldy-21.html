Baldy 21

Baldy 21 is the CSE Introduction to Programming Instructional Lab.

Baldy 21 hosts Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) introduction to programming course sequence undergraduate and graduate class labs/course recitations.

  • View Large Map

Baldy 21 Calendar

Fact Sheet

  • Dimensions: 42.08' (north-south) x 20.17' (east-west)
  • Area: 822.78 net square feet (nsf)
  • Workstations: Twenty-nine (29) Dell Optiplex 7020 workstations, running the CentOS operating system, whose hostnames are named after Game of Thrones characters
  • Videoprojection: Three (3) Sharp 60" LCD flat panel monitors mounted on the south and west walls, managed by Altona At-Line-Pro5-Gen2 video presentation system (AV Solutions, Oct 2014)
  • Swipe Card Access: Granted to CSE faculty and undergraduate teaching assistants (UTAs) who teach labs and recitations hosted here.
  • Most recently rehabbed: 2014

Courses

Over the last five years, Baldy 21 has hosted class labs/course recitations for these courses:

  • CSE 115—Introduction to Computer Science I (Spring 2026, Fall 2025, Spring 2025, Fall 2024, Spring 2024, Fall 2023, Spring 2023, Fall 2022, Spring 2022, Fall 2021)
  • CSE 503—Computer Science for Non Majors (Fall 2024, Fall 2023, Fall 2022, Fall 2021)
