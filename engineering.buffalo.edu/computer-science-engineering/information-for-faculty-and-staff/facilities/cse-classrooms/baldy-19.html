Baldy 19

Baldy 19 is the CSE Software Engineering Instructional Lab.  It hosts CSE programming and software engineering course recitations.

  • View Large Map

Fact Sheet

  • Area: 1,140.50 net square feet (nsf)
  • Workstations: Two Ergotron Zip40 Charging and Management Carts containing 25 MacBook Air 15" 2023 laptops, running MacOS, named cse-loaner-[101-125].cse.buffalo.edu.  One laptop charging cart is stationary and serves Baldy 19; the other roves and serves CSE classes in outlying classrooms.
  • Videoprojection: 8 flat panel monitors mounted on the north and south walls.
  • Swipe Card Access: Yes. Granted to CSE faculty and undergraduate teaching assistants (UTAs) who teach recitations hosted here.
  • Most recently rehabbed: 2019

Courses

Over the last five years, Baldy 19 has hosted class labs/course recitations for these courses:

  • CSE 116—Introduction to Computer Science II (Spring 2026, Fall 2025, Spring 2025, Fall 2024, Spring 2024, Fall 2023)
  • CSE 220—Systems Programming (Spring 2024, Spring 2023, Fall 2022, Spring 2022, Fall 2021)
  • CSE 250—Data Structures (Spring 2023)
  • CSE 306—Software Quality in Practice (Fall 2025, Fall 2024)
  • CSE 331—Algorithms and Complexity (Spring 2023)
  • CSE 442—Software Engineering (Spring 2026, Fall 2025, Spring 2025, Fall 2024, Spring 2024, Fall 2023, Spring 2023, Fall 2022, Fall 2021)
